Why VaaniPay?

Payments Built for
Real People

No jargon. No complexity. Fast, simple payments that work for everyone – including first-time smartphone users.

Voice-Based Payments

Say "Send 200 rupees to Suresh" – no forms, no UPI IDs. Works naturally in your own words and dialect.

Multilingual Interface

Speak in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Kannada, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi and more – 12 languages supported.

Secure Transactions

Our Story

We Built VaaniPay Because
India Deserves Better

In 2021, our founders watched a street vendor in Varanasi struggle for 10 minutes trying to accept a digital payment — fumbling with a phone, unable to read English menus, asking younger customers for help. That moment sparked VaaniPay.

We're a team of engineers, linguists, and designers from across India — Bengaluru, Chennai, Patna, Jaipur — united by one belief: every Indian, regardless of literacy, language, or location, deserves access to the digital economy.

Today, VaaniPay serves 2.4 million users across 22 states, from Nagaland to Kerala, processing over ₹480 crore in transactions. Our voice-first, multilingual platform has become the payment method of choice for farmers, street vendors, domestic workers, and first-generation smartphone users.
